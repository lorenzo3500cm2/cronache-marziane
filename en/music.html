Music

Cronache Marziane develops activities related to music, sound and experimentation, through programmes, productions, listening sessions, meetings and collaborations with artists, musicians and cultural spaces.

A biographical and musical dossier on Mino Di Martino, from I Giganti to experimental research, from Telaio Magnetico to Albergo Intergalattico Spaziale, and his collaborations with Franco Battiato, Alice and other figures of Italian music.

Sound archive

Materials, recordings, documents and research paths connected to music, sound culture, performance and experimental practices.

Programmes and productions

Concerts, listening formats, audiovisual projects and interdisciplinary productions that explore the relationship between music, image, text, memory and contemporary artistic research.
